fwupdmgr NOTES

  • df -h                    <---get efi mount
    • 
    
  • nano /etc/fwupd/daemon.conf
    • 
    
  • Esplocation=/boot/efi    <---add efi mount location
    • 
    
  • systemctl restart fwupd.service
    • 
    
  • Re-Run fwupdmgr
